Verdict

The Load-Following feature ships only in the Electric/MMT WIP template — a new hedge-strip workbench (Hedges + IMA-Hedges sheets, CommodityHedges/HedgeHistory/DNT_INPUT_Hedges tables, 3 new VBA UDFs, 2 new submit-payload keys). The gas WIP templates carry zero LF machinery: Gas Choice WIP is functionally identical to Live (0 formula deltas across 50 sheets, byte-identical VBA source in 77/77 modules), and GTS WIP is Live plus exactly one formula change — a margin-calculation SUMIF anchor whose intended direction is unconfirmed.

Two findings deserve escalation before LF deals go live: the GTS V27 anchor question (Live excludes contract months 1–24 from the IGS-Margin sum; WIP includes them — one of the two is mis-summing margin) and the Electric Hedges!H17 dev note ("35 will need changed to 23 OR 21 - Broker fees") sitting unresolved next to the Modify-LF current-margin VLOOKUP.

Findings

High H1 · GTS IGS-Margin SUMIF anchor — Live and WIP disagree; one of them is mis-summing margin

Pricing Sheet!V27:V32 — Live: SUMIF($A$87:$A$171,"<="&F27,$E$87:$E$171); WIP: SUMIF($A$63:$A$171,…,$E$63:$E$171). Both templates carry the identical month grid at A63:A171 (A63 = month 1, A87 = month 25), so Live's anchor excludes the first 24 contract months from the "IGS Margin $" sum; WIP includes them. This is the only template-level difference on the GTS path — it reads like a margin-calc fix riding in WIP, but the direction is unconfirmed.

  • Action: ask Structuring which anchor is intended. If WIP is the fix → Live GTS sheets are under-summing margin today. If Live is intended → the WIP "fix" is a regression waiting to ship.

High H2 · Electric WIP dev-note residue — unresolved column index in Modify-LF margin math

Hedges!H17 = "<- 35 will need changed to 23 OR 21 - Broker fees", sitting next to the G17 VLOOKUP into IMA-ContractDetailInfo with B17=35. If 35 is the wrong column, Modify-LF current-margin math is wrong in the template that LF deals will use.

  • Action: surface to CC/Structuring; resolve the 35-vs-23/21 question before Modify+LF deals price through this sheet.

Medium M1 · GetLocationVolumes breaking signature change (6→4 args)

The Electric WIP drops the Rack As String, Multiplier As Double params; ExhibitA call sites were rewritten, but the rack-rate ExhibitHelp!AG2:AH13 range is now orphaned. Anything outside this workbook still calling the 6-arg form breaks; rack-rate handling in exhibit volume generation silently disappeared.

Medium M2 · Scraper contract unverified for the two new submit keys (CI-7513)

Upload rows 220–221 (CommodityHedges JSON + MonthlyMargin) are new in the Electric WIP. Whether the scraper / Pending.Hedges pipeline reads these keys is unverified — if not, LF submits would silently drop hedge data at the Drop-folder seam.

Medium M3 · No version metadata exists in any template

No version cell, no "WIP"/"Load Following" string, identical docProps/calcId; both gas templates share a 2004-08-11 creation lineage. Gas Choice WIP is literally undeterminable in-file. The KB now carries the best-available per-path tells (below); the durable fix is a template-version custom doc property stamped at generation.
